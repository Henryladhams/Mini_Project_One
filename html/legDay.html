Leg Day

Welcome to Leg Day! This is the third day of your training routine. On this day, you will focus on exercises that target your quads, hamstrings, glutes and calves.

Here are the exercises you will be doing today:

  • Back Squat: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • RDL: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Seated Hamstring Curls: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Back Squat

  • Works the quads, glutes and calves
  • Take three seconds to squat down to max depth
  • Hold for one second at the bottom
  • Stand up with max force then hold for one second at the top before your next rep

RDL

  • Works the hamgstrings, glutes and back
  • Take three seconds to reach down as low as you can go
  • Hold for one second at the bottom
  • Pull back up with max force then hold for one second at the top before your next rep

Leg Extensions

  • Works the quads
  • Fully extend your legs at max force
  • Hold for one second
  • Retract your legs back down for three seconds until they're fully retracted, hold for one second then explode back up for your next rep

Seated Hamstring Curls

  • Works the hamgstrings
  • Retract your legs at full force
  • Once fully retracted hold for one second
  • Extend your legs for three seconds until fully extended, hold for one second then retract with full force for your next rep

Calf Raises

  • Works the calves
  • Stand up on the balls of your feet at max force
  • Hold for one second at full extension
  • Descend for three seconds, hold for one second at the bottom then explode back up

Make sure to warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterwards with some stretching. Good luck!
