Pull Day

Welcome to Pull Day! This is the second day of your training routine. On this day, you will focus on exercises that target your back, forearms and biceps.

Here are the exercises you will be doing today:

  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bent Over Row: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Pull Downs: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Rotating Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Pull-Ups

  • Works your lats, upper back, biceps and forearms
  • Pull up at max force
  • Hold for one second at the top
  • Lower yourself down for three seconds, hold for one second then explode back up for your next rep

Bent Over Row

  • Works your upper back, lats, rear delts and forearms
  • Pull up at max force
  • Hold for one second at the top
  • Lower the weight down for three seconds, hold for one second at the bottom then explode back up for your next rep

Lateral Pull Downs

  • Works your lats, upper back, biceps and forearms
  • Pull down at max force
  • Hold for one second at the bottom
  • Lower the weight back up for three seconds, hold for one second at the top then explode back down for your next rep

Seated Cable Rows

  • Works your upper back, lats, rear delts and forearms
  • Pull back at max force
  • Hold for one second
  • Fully extend for three seconds, hold for one second at full extension then explosively pull back for your next rep

Bicep Curls

  • Works your biceps and forearms
  • Pull the weight up explosively
  • Hold and squeeze for one second at the top with full contraction
  • Lower the weight down for three seconds, hold for one second at the bottom then explode back up for your next rep

Make sure to warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterwards with some stretching. Good luck!
