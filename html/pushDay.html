Push Day

Welcome to Push Day! This is the first day of your training routine. On this day, you will focus on exercises that target your chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Here are the exercises you will be doing today:

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Military Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Chest Fly Machine: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Bench Press

  • Works your chest, upper back, triceps and front delts
  • Push up with max force
  • Hold for one second at the top
  • Descend for three seconds, hold for one second at the bottom then explode back up for your next rep

Military Press

  • Works your delts, triceps and core
  • explosively push the weight up
  • Hold at the top for one second
  • Bring the weight back down for three seconds, hold at the bottom and then explode back up for your next rep

Chest Fly Machine

  • Works your chest
  • Bring your arms together explosively
  • Hold for one second
  • Retract your arms back for three seconds, hold for one second once at full retraction, then bring your arms back together explosively for your next rep

Tricep Dips

  • Works your triceps and lower chest
  • Descend for three seconds
  • Hold for one second at the bottom
  • Explode back up and hold one second at the top before descending again

Lateral Raises

  • Works your delts
  • Raise the weights by your sides explosively
  • Hold for one second at the top
  • Lower the weights back down for three seconds, hold for one second at the bottom then explode back up

Make sure to warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterwards with some stretching. Good luck!
